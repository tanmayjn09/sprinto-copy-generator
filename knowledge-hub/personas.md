# Sprinto - Target ICP & Personas

## Primary ICP
B2B SaaS companies, 20-500 employees, Series A to Series C, selling to enterprise customers who require compliance certifications as a procurement condition.

## Segments
- **Startups** - Fast, simple compliance that unblocks enterprise deals
- **Mid-market** - Scalable compliance for growing teams
- **Enterprise / CISOs** - Real-time visibility and control across frameworks
- **IT Teams** - Reduce manual GRC workload
- **GRC Teams** - Multi-framework management in one place

## Industries
- SaaS (primary)
- BFSI - financial services
- Healthcare / Pharma
- Any regulated industry

## Buyer personas
1. **Founder/CTO at seed-Series B SaaS** - blocked on enterprise deal, needs SOC 2 fast, doesn't want to pull engineers off product
2. **IT/Security Manager** - drowning in manual evidence collection, needs automation
3. **CISO at scaling company** - needs real-time visibility, multi-framework management, board-level reporting
4. **GRC Manager** - running compliance across multiple frameworks, needs a single platform

## Trigger events (when they're ready to buy)
- Enterprise prospect asks for SOC 2 before signing
- First compliance audit coming up
- Engineers complaining about time spent on compliance
- Expanding to EU (GDPR) or healthcare (HIPAA)
- Bad experience with another tool (Vanta/Drata too complex or expensive)
- Needing multiple frameworks at once
