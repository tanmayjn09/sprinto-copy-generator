# Sprinto - Competitor Differentiation

## Vs. Vanta
- Sprinto is simpler to use (#1 Ease of Use on G2 vs Vanta's complexity)
- Faster implementation - weeks vs months
- More guided - 1:1 expert sessions vs Vanta's self-serve model
- More affordable - Vanta is known to be expensive at scale
- Continuous compliance, not just audit-time compliance
- Customer proof: Intellect explicitly switched FROM Vanta TO Sprinto

## Vs. Drata
- Sprinto has deeper integrations (200+)
- More affordable at growth stage
- Better multi-framework support in one platform
- Stronger guided onboarding and support

## Vs. Secureframe
- Faster time to certification
- More automation (80% vs manual-heavy Secureframe)
- Broader framework coverage

## Angles competitors are NOT owning (use these)
- Speed to certification (2 weeks is uniquely Sprinto)
- 100% audit success rate - no competitor claims this
- Multi-framework simultaneously (SOC 2 + ISO + HIPAA + GDPR at once)
- "Like having a team member" - human + tech hybrid vs pure self-serve
- Engineer time saved - nobody else leads with this
- Continuous compliance (staying compliant, not just getting compliant)

## What to avoid
- Do not copy Vanta's "security compliance" general framing
- Do not copy Drata's "modern compliance" language
- Do not use "streamline" or "simplify" - every competitor uses these
