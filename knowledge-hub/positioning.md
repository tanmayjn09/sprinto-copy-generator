# Sprinto - Core Positioning

## Primary message
Autonomous Trust Platform for Compliance, Risk & GRC

## Taglines (use these as hooks or proof anchors)
- "Always audit-ready, never manual"
- "SOC 2 minus the struggle"
- "Don't just get compliant. Stay compliant."
- "Get compliant in as little as 2 weeks"

## Core value prop
Sprinto eliminates manual compliance work. Companies get SOC 2, ISO 27001, GDPR, HIPAA certified fast - and stay certified year-round - without pulling engineers or ops off real work.

## Key pain points we solve
- Enterprise deal blocked pending SOC 2 or ISO certification
- Engineering team buried in manual evidence collection
- First audit coming up, no internal expertise
- Compliance lapses between audits (point-in-time tools fail here)
- Multi-framework complexity - needing SOC 2 + ISO + GDPR + HIPAA at once
- Switching from a tool that was too complex or too expensive

## What we stand for
- Speed - weeks not months
- Automation - 90% of tasks automated
- Guided - expert hand-holding, not self-serve
- Continuous - always on, not just at audit time
- Simple - #1 in Ease of Use on G2
